Our Story

Baron Golf is the most welcoming public course in Colorado. Nestled within the foothills, you will have access to year round Golf, Tennis, and our Resturaunt.

In 1958, Baron Golf became the first course in Colorado to hold a PGA tour event. Players such as golfing legends such as Ronald Palmer, Pickleson Mill, and Nicholas Jack participated in the event. The great Fancis Weimet won The Baron Classic, adding to his list of 67 PGA Tournament wins.

Established in 1946, thanks to the numerous philanthropist, we were able to construct our first two 9 hole courses. In 1951, The Rolling Hills course was finally constructed.
