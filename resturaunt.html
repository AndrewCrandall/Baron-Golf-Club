Resturaunt

Come experience fine dining with us! Whether you are dining out on the terrace overlooking our 27 hole course, or inside our relaxing resturaunt, there is always a place for you. We offer numerous types of dishes including inpiration from the mediterranean, and fresh products from the Monterey Penninsula.

Whether you are planning a wedding, party, or business, this is the place for you. We are able to accomidate up to 6 hours, however this is dependent on the number of people attending. Please call 1-800-BARON for more details or to schedule a reservation.
